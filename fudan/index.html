In order to strengthen the academic exchanges and cooperation on mathematics of well-known universities in east asia , present the latest research results in the areas of mathematics, and promote academic research in mathematics,and related disciplinaries, the East Asian Core Doctorial forum on Mathematics provide sustainable exchange platform for students and experts.The forum is initiated by nine universities including Kyoto University, Tohoku University, The University of Tokyo, Seoul National University, Sungkyunkwan University, Ewha Women's University, Fudan University, Tsinghua University and National Taiwan University.

This year the forum will be hosted by Fudan University, school of Mathematical Science (SMS), The SMS consists of five Departments, i.e.,Departments of Pure Mathematics, Applied Mathematics, Information & Computing Sciences, Mathematical Finance & Control Theory, and Probability, Statistics & Actuarial Sciences, and the Institute of Mathematics. In the past six decades, led by SU Buqing, CHEN Jiangong, and GU Chaohao, the world-renowned mathematicians, the SMS has not only achieved an excellent research reputation in the community of mathematics but also brought up numerous elites in various scientific, technological, or industrial domains, including 14 academicians of Chinese Academy of Sciences, 5 academicians of Chinese Academy of Engineering, and a large number of scientists and engineers of great achievements.
